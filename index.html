Our services

Consultancy for Utilities Contractors & Network Operators

We support utilities contractors and network operators across the full lifecycle of fibre and utility infrastructure delivery — from early strategy and mobilisation through to build, assurance and operational optimisation.

Our consultancy focuses on practical delivery, not theory. We work directly with client teams to design operating models, improve productivity, reduce risk and ensure programmes remain commercially and operationally controlled as they scale.

Typical areas of support include:

  • End-to-end programme and portfolio management for large-scale network deployments
  • Mobilisation support for new contracts, frameworks and BDUK-funded programmes
  • Resource modelling, productivity benchmarking and delivery forecasting
  • Target Operating Model (TOM) design across planning, build, QA and close-out
  • Risk identification, mitigation planning and governance frameworks
  • Deployment strategy, build methodology selection and sequencing
  • Independent assurance, performance reviews and recovery planning

Our experience spans rural and urban fibre builds, working directly with delivery partners, local authorities and stakeholders to ensure programmes are realistic, compliant and executable on the ground — not just in a board pack.

Software Products

Glidepath is an operational and project management platform that gives full visibility and control of live utility works. This easy-to-integrate system replaces spreadsheets and disconnected tools with a single operational view — helping teams reduce delays, minimise FPNs and improve delivery certainty.

GeoProx is a digital risk assessment platform built specifically to support compliance with the Street Works UK (SWUK) protocol. It automates desktop proximity risk assessments and provides mobile-friendly site risk assessment tools, ensuring risks are identified, recorded and managed consistently in line with SWUK requirements.
