Our services

Consultancy for Utilities Contractors & Network Operators

We support utilities contractors and network operators across the full lifecycle of fibre and utility infrastructure delivery — from early strategy and mobilisation through to build, assurance and operational optimisation.

Our consultancy focuses on practical delivery, not theory. We work directly with client teams to design operating models, improve productivity, reduce risk and ensure programmes remain commercially and operationally controlled as they scale.

Typical areas of support include:

  • End-to-end programme and portfolio management for large-scale network deployments
  • Mobilisation support for new contracts, frameworks and BDUK-funded programmes
  • Resource modelling, productivity benchmarking and delivery forecasting
  • Target Operating Model (TOM) design across planning, build, QA and close-out
  • Risk identification, mitigation planning and governance frameworks
  • Deployment strategy, build methodology selection and sequencing
  • Independent assurance, performance reviews and recovery planning

Our experience spans rural and urban fibre builds, working directly with delivery partners, local authorities and stakeholders to ensure programmes are realistic, compliant and executable on the ground — not just in a board pack.

Software Products

Our software platforms are built specifically for the utilities and fibre deployment sector, informed by real-world delivery experience and the challenges faced by operational and commercial teams.

Each product is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing systems, reduce manual admin and provide clear, auditable data from the field through to senior management.

Glidepath

Glidepath is an operational and project management platform that gives full visibility and control of live utility works.

Key capabilities include:

  • Issuing job packs and work instructions directly to field teams
  • End-to-end permit management linked to Street Manager
  • GIS integration for planning, live progress and historical records
  • Real-time tracking of works, delays, risks and key milestones
  • Capture of site evidence, photos and QA records
  • Improved coordination of traffic management, crews and resources

Glidepath replaces spreadsheets and disconnected tools with a single operational view — helping teams reduce delays, minimise FPNs and improve delivery certainty.

GeoProx

GeoProx is a SWUK-aligned risk assessment platform designed to simplify and automate compliance.

Key capabilities include:

  • Automated desktop proximity risk assessments
  • Digital site risk assessments and inspections
  • Permit document storage and audit-ready records
  • Sampling regime management and results capture
  • Exportable reports and PDFs for authority submissions
  • Mobile-friendly tools for field teams

GeoProx reduces administrative burden while ensuring consistent, compliant risk management across all works — supporting safer delivery and clearer audit trails.
